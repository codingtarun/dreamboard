## Key Research Findings

- **43% of users prefer offline music sources** — validated demand
- **Offline playback = #1 most desired feature** (30.8% of feature importance)
- Android holds **42.3% of music app market share**
- Revenue models: Free + one-time Pro ($2-5) is standard; donations are unreliable

## Competitor Landscape

| App | Model | Their Weakness (Our Opportunity) |
|-----|-------|--------------------------------|
| Musicolet | Free, no ads | Ugly/utilitarian UI |
| Poweramp | $4.99 | Overwhelming for casual users |
| BlackPlayer | Free + $3.49 EX | Ads in free version |
| Pulsar | Free + $2.99+ | Basic EQ locked behind paywall |
| Oto Music | Free, open-source | Very niche, limited features |

**The gap:** A player with **Musicolet's power + Pulsar's beauty + true zero-permission privacy**.
