## The Spark
A dedicated space to dump every crazy idea without judgment — from app concepts to life experiments to wild business ideas. A personal idea management system where an AI curator keeps things organized, connected, and alive.

## Details
- Folder-based status workflow (raw → developing → incubating → completed/archive)
- Markdown files for human readability + AI manageability
- Tagging and cross-linking between ideas
- Spark level for gut-feeling priority
- Notes log for tracking evolution over time
