## The Spark

India's domestic travel market is **exploding**:
- 2+ billion domestic trips annually
- Government pushing infrastructure (airports, highways, railways)
- Simplified visas increasing foreign arrivals
- Rising middle class with disposable income
- Yet most travel apps are **international-first**, ignoring India's diversity

**Bhatko India** = A travel platform built **by Indians, for Indian travelers** (and India-curious foreigners).

---

## The Concept

India has 28 states, 8 union territories, 22 official languages, infinite sub-cultures. No generic app can capture this.

### Niche Verticals Within Bhatko India

| Vertical | What It Offers |
|----------|---------------|
| **Bhatko Heritage** | Forts, palaces, UNESCO sites, heritage walks |
| **Bhatko Bhraman** | Spiritual journeys (Char Dham, Buddhist circuit, Sufi trails) |
| **Bhatko Swad** | Food trails — city-wise street food maps, home dining |
| **Bhatko Trek** | Himalayan treks, Western Ghats, Nilgiris |
| **Bhatko Beaches** | Andaman, Goa, Gokarna, Varkala — beyond the crowds |
| **Bhatko Village** | Rural tourism, farm stays, craft villages |
| **Bhatko Festivals** | Event-based travel (Holi in Mathura, Durga Puja in Kolkata, etc.) |

### USP
- **Regional language support** (Hindi, Tamil, Bengali, etc.)
- **Local pricing** — no foreign tourist pricing
- **Indian payment methods** — UPI, Paytm, PhonePe
- **Train integration** — IRCTC is India's real travel backbone
- **Festival calendar** — auto-suggests trips around festivals

## Why It Excites Me

- **Enormous TAM** — India's domestic market alone is massive
- **Government support** — tourism infrastructure booming
- **Cultural depth** — impossible for global players to replicate authentically
- **Data moat** — localized knowledge is defensible

## Potential Obstacles

- Fragmented supply (thousands of small hotels, transport operators)
- Regional complexity (languages, customs, infrastructure)
- Seasonality (some destinations only viable 3-4 months/year)

## Next Steps
