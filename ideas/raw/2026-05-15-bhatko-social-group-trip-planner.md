## The Spark

Planning a trip with friends is a **nightmare**:
- "Where should we go?" → 50 messages, no decision
- "What's the budget?" → Everyone has different ideas
- "Who's booking what?" → Chaos
- "Did you send me the money?" → Awkward reminders

**Bhatko Social** = The antidote to group trip planning hell.

---

## The Concept

Group trip planning app that eliminates friction:

### Features
1. **Destination Voting** — Everyone suggests/votes on destinations
2. **Budget Consensus** — Anonymous budget input → auto-suggests median
3. **Itinerary Collaboration** — Shared itinerary, drag-drop planning
4. **Auto-Split Payments** — Integrated expense splitting + reminders
5. **Bhatko Reveal** — Surprise trip option: one person plans, others discover on arrival
6. **Group Chat + Polls** — Built-in, no WhatsApp clutter
7. **Shared Packing List** — Who's bringing what

### The "Bhatko Reveal" Feature
One friend plans the entire surprise trip. Others only know:
- Dates
- Packing list hints
- Budget
- Vibe tags ("beach," "adventure," "chill")

They discover the destination at the airport. Pure magic.

---

## Why It Excites Me

- Group travel is **huge** (bachelor trips, reunions, friend-cations)
- Current solutions (Google Docs, WhatsApp, Splitwise) are fragmented
- "Bhatko Reveal" is a **viral feature** — TikTok/Instagram gold
- Can integrate with main Bhatko booking engine

## Potential Obstacles

- Need network effects — useless if friends don't all install
- Group dynamics are complex (one person dominates, flakes, etc.)
- Payment disputes can get ugly
