## Prompt Crafting Tips
- **Be specific:** Instead of "a person," say "a young South Asian professional in a modern coworking space."
- **Name the style:** "Cinematic lighting," "corporate flat vector illustration," "hyper-realistic 3D render."
- **Specify camera/lens** for photorealistic images: "shot on Sony A7IV, 35mm lens, f/1.8."
- **Use negative prompts** if your generator supports them: `--no text, watermark, blurry, deformed.`
