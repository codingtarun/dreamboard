## Prompt 1: Conceptual / Blog Hero (16:9)

A dramatic split-screen digital illustration of a human developer and an AI coding agent working together. On the left, a focused developer sits at a glowing holographic workstation, surrounded by floating data structures, algorithms, and system architecture diagrams that illuminate their face with warm golden light. On the right, a sleek, ethereal AI entity made of flowing code streams and neural network patterns extends a hand toward the developer—not replacing them, but collaborating. Cinematic lighting, deep blues and golds, hyper-detailed, editorial illustration style, 8k quality. Aspect ratio 16:9.
