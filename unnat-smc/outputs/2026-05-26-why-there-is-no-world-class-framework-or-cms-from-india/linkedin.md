**Topic:** Why There Is No World-Class Framework or CMS From India
**Date:** 2026-05-26
**Word Count:** ~210 words

---

🇮🇳 India produces 1.5M+ engineers every year.

We power the backends of Google, Microsoft, and Meta.

But name ONE world-class open-source framework or CMS built from India.

I'll wait.

---

This isn't a talent problem. It's a **mindset problem**.

For decades, Indian tech was trained to **serve**, not to **build**.

- IT services culture rewarded billable hours, not bold products
- Risk aversion killed long-term R&D bets
- Open source was seen as "free work," not ecosystem building
- Our best builders often left for Silicon Valley

The result? We became the world's **backoffice**, not its **backbone**.

But the tide is turning.

Projects like **Hoppscotch** (API dev), **ERPNext** (open-source ERP), and **Bagisto** (e-commerce) are proving Indian builders can ship globally.

The real question: will we stay consumers of Western frameworks, or become creators of our own?
