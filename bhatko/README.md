## What is Bhatko?

Bhatko (भटको) means "wander" or "get lost" in Hindi — the perfect brand for a **spontaneous travel platform**.

The problem: Travel planning takes 10+ hours. Decision fatigue kills the magic.

The solution: Set your budget and dates, let AI curate a complete surprise trip — flights, stay, experiences — booked in 60 seconds.
