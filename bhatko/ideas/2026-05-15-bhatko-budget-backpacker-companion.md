## The Spark

Budget travelers — backpackers, students, digital nomads — have specific needs that mainstream apps ignore:
- Hostels over hotels
- Free/cheap activities
- Shared rides & transport
- Meeting other travelers
- Splitting costs
- Offline maps for remote areas

**Bhatko Budget** = The ultimate companion for travelers who'd rather spend on experiences than rooms.

---

## The Concept

All-in-one app for budget & solo travelers:

### Features
1. **Hostel Finder** — Curated hostels with vibes (party / chill / work / eco)
2. **Bhatko Rides** — Ride-sharing with other travelers (cheaper than taxis)
3. **Free & Cheap** — Free walking tours, public beaches, street food maps, free museums
4. **Travel Buddies** — Match with compatible solo travelers
5. **Expense Splitter** — Split costs with travel buddies in real-time
6. **Offline Mode** — Download city guides, maps, translations for offline use
7. **Bhatko Stays** — Homestays, couchsurfing-style stays, volunteer-for-room

### Target Users
- 18-30 year old backpackers
- Solo female travelers (safety features)
- Digital nomads on tight budgets
- Gap year students

---

## Why It Excites Me

- Massive underserved market (millions of backpackers globally)
- Strong community potential
- Low CAC — word of mouth in hostels is powerful
- Can monetize through hostel commissions, affiliate bookings, premium features

## Potential Obstacles

- Safety concerns for solo travelers (especially women)
- Hostel partnerships take time to build
- Budget users have low LTV — need volume
