## The Spark

Airbnb Experiences exists, but it's **touristy**. What about the *real* local stuff?

A chaiwallah in Old Delhi who makes the best masala chai. A grandmother in Kerala who teaches authentic cooking. A street artist in Berlin who knows every graffiti wall. A fisherman in Goa who takes you to secret beaches.

**Bhatko Local** = A marketplace where locals host authentic, off-beat experiences that tourists would never find on TripAdvisor.

---

## The Concept

Platform connecting travelers with **verified local hosts** for:
- Food walks & home dining
- Craft workshops (pottery, weaving, block printing)
- Adventure with locals (trekking, fishing, cycling)
- Cultural immersion (festivals, rituals, ceremonies)
- Hidden spots only locals know

### Differentiation from Airbnb Experiences
| | Airbnb Experiences | Bhatko Local |
|---|---|---|
| Host type | Professionals & semi-pros | Everyday locals |
| Pricing | $30-200+ | $5-50 (accessible) |
| Vibe | Polished, tourist-friendly | Raw, authentic, unscripted |
| Discovery | Search-based | AI-matched to traveler personality |
| Focus | Global cities | Tier-2 cities & villages |

---

## Why It Excites Me

- Democratizes tourism income to **real locals**, not just hotel chains
- Perfect for the **"authentic travel"** trend (huge in 2026)
- Low startup cost — marketplace model, no inventory
- Strong **ESG/sustainability** angle
- Can launch as a **feature within main Bhatko app**

## Potential Obstacles

- Host verification & safety
- Quality control without killing authenticity
- Language barriers
- Insurance/liability for experiences

## Next Steps

- Validate demand with 10 traveler interviews
- Find 5 pilot hosts in one city (e.g., Jaipur or Goa)
- Build a simple landing page + booking form
