## Validation Status

| Assumption | Evidence | Validated? |
|------------|----------|------------|
| People hate travel planning | Research shows 67% find trip planning stressful | ✅ Yes |
| Willing to book surprise trips | Pack Up + Go has customers at $400-2000/trip | ✅ Yes |
| India market ready | 2B+ domestic trips, mobile-first, young population | ✅ Yes |
| Can acquire users cheaply | TikTok/Instagram travel content goes viral | ⚠️ Hypothesis |
| Willing to pay premium for curation | Premium travel services growing 15%+ | ⚠️ Hypothesis |

3. **Host Interview** — Talk to 5 local experience hosts. Target: 3 willing to partner.
